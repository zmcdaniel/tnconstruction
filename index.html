About

Bond's Cay

Out island sustainability

A 700 acre private island focused on sustainable, residential development. Bruce managed civil design, approval, permitting, including securing the first government approvals for an alternate infrastructure system. Taking advantage of advances in non-fossil fuel energy technology supply, our system's net result is a sustainable model with virtually zero developer infrastructure cost.

Utilizing a blend of foreign material supply and local labor, we established a vertical construction division capable of delivering custom, high-end residences on a U.S. schedule with a level 5 finish despite the challenges presented by building on a remote, uninhabited island. The design integrated the highest level of sustainability coupled with independent, non-fossil fuel energy supply at a low environmental impact market price point.

Collaborating with a Spanish design group, Bruce succeeded in bidding and acquiring first permits for Penelope Cruz's custom residence.

  • Date: 2012

Conch House

Luxury villa

Located adjacent to the Marina Village at Baker's Bay, this charming beachfront home is situated on the beach overlooking the Atlantic Ocean and is minutes from all amenities in the Marina Village.

The nearly 1 acre homesite has 95 feet of frontage on the Atlantic and backs up to the boat slips in the Baker's Bay Marina.

The home features a main house with master suite, two guest suites, bunk room, second floor sitting area, great room with a bar, kitchen, laundry and pantry. The property also includes a guest cabana with two bedrooms, two full baths, kitchen and laundry.

Features include expansive verandas on both levels, an indoor great room fireplace, a master bath indoor to outdoor shower, high end appliances and finishes and an infinity edge swimming pool with spa.
